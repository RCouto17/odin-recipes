Gnocchi

Description

The second best food in the world, hands down

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil; add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes.
    Drain, cool, and mash with a fork or potato masher.

  2. Combine 1 cup mashed potato, flour, and egg in a large bowl. Knead until dough forms a ball. Shape small portions of the dough into long "snakes".
    On a floured surface, cut snakes into 1/2-inch pieces.

  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    Drop in gnocchi and cook for 3 to 5 minutes or until gnocchi have risen to the top; drain and serve.
